## Private Domain Registration FAQ

This FAQ addresses common questions about private domain registration, WHOIS privacy services, and GDPR protections. For comprehensive coverage, see the [Private Domain Registration guide](/en/library/private-domain-registration/).

### WHOIS Privacy

**What is WHOIS privacy?**

WHOIS privacy is a service that replaces your actual contact information in public WHOIS/RDAP lookups with proxy contact details. The privacy service provider forwards legitimate communications to you while protecting your personal data from public exposure.

**Is WHOIS privacy free?**

Some registrars include WHOIS privacy at no additional cost, while others charge an annual fee. The availability and pricing of privacy services varies by registrar and TLD.

### GDPR and Domain Data

**How does GDPR affect domain registration?**

GDPR restricts the public display of EU registrants' personal data in WHOIS. ICANN's Temporary Specification adapted the WHOIS system to GDPR requirements, limiting public access to registrant data while preserving accredited access for legitimate purposes.
