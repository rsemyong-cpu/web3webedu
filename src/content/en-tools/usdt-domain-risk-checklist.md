## Summary

This checklist helps users complete basic verifications before payment, reducing risks of misdirected transfers, timeouts, refund difficulties, and domain control loss.

## How to Use

Before completing a USDT domain purchase, go through each check item below. Record the confirmation result and timestamp. Re-check if the registrar updates policies or if the payment environment changes.

## Checklist

| Check Item | What to Confirm | How to Record |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Payment method | Whether USDT, BTC, USDC or third-party gateway is supported | Record official page and update timestamp |
| Chain and fees | Whether TRC-20, ERC-20, Polygon etc. are available | Record chain, network fee, and confirmation time |
| Identity and invoicing | Whether KYC is required, whether corporate invoices supported | Record applicable scenarios |
| Domain security | Whether WHOIS privacy, DNSSEC, Registry Lock supported | Record TLD restrictions |
| Dispute resolution | Refund, failed payment, domain freeze, and appeal pathways | Record support channels and terms links |

## Risks and Limitations

This checklist covers common verification items only and cannot address every edge case. Registrar policies, blockchain network conditions, and regulatory requirements may change at any time. Always refer to the latest official terms.

## Compliance Boundaries

This checklist is for risk education and reference only. It must not be used to facilitate fraud, infringement, regulatory evasion, sanction circumvention, or other illegal purposes. Users are responsible for ensuring their domain registration and payment activities comply with applicable laws.
